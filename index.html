Welcome to the Jensen Lab in the Biomedical Engineering Department at the University of Michigan. We combine laboratory robotics with artificial intelligence to solve fundamental problems in microbiology. Our self-driving lab has performed over 1 million high-throughput experiments to understand bacterial metabolism and quorum sensing.

  • Meet our people and our robots.
  • Read our publications and preprints.
  • Find teaching materials including our undergraduate textbook Linear Algebra: Foundations of Machine Learning.
  • Learn about our workshops on autonomous science. We hold workshops around the world at conferences, companies, national laboratories, foundations, and academic institutions.
