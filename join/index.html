Join the Jensen Lab!

We are recruiting graduate students (M.S. and Ph.D.) for Fall 2021 in the following areas:
  • Deep Reinforcement Learning
  • Automated/Combinatorial Science
  • High-throughput Genomic Technologies
Students with backgrounds in engineering, computer science, and microbiology can join our multi-disciplinary team as we tackle big questions about the oral microbiome. Check out our research or state-of-the-art robotic systems.

How to apply:

We accept students through the following graduate programs:
  • Bioengineering
  • Microbiology
  • Informatics
Both M.S. and Ph.D. students receive financial support as research assistants (RAs). Our work is funded by the NIGMS, NIBIB, NSF, and Sandia National Lab.
