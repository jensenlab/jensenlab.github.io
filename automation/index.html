Automation Hardware

Our lab uses state-of-the-art hardware to tackle large and complex biological problems. Our current automation system includes the following instruments.

Measurement & Detection

Cytation 10 Automated Microscope/Multimode Plate Reader

Agilent

  • 20x-40x confocal imaging (60 um spinning disk)
  • 4x-40x phase contrast imaging
  • 1.25x-40x brightfield imaging
  • Sony CMOS, 16-bit grayscale camera
  • 6- to 1536-well plate reading
  • monochromator fluorescence (variable bandwidth)
  • filter-dichroic fluorescence
  • monochromator UV-Vis absorbance
  • luminescence
  • aerobic & anaerobic incubation
  • dual reagent injectors
Cytation 5 Multimode Plate Reader

Agilent

  • 6- to 1536-well plate reading
  • monochromator fluorescence (variable bandwidth)
  • filter-dichroic fluorescence
  • monochromator UV-Vis absorbance
  • luminescence
  • aerobic & anaerobic incubation
  • dual reagent injectors
  • pH-XTRA glycolysis module
Synergy H1 Multimode Plate Reader (x2)

Agilent

  • 6- to 384-well plate reading
  • monochromator fluorescence (variable bandwidth)
  • monochromator UV-Vis absorbance
  • luminescence
  • temperature control
  • linear, orbital, and double orbital shaking
Epoch 2 Plate Reader (x3)

Agilent

  • monochromator UV-Vis absorbance
  • 6- to 384-well plate reading
  • incubation and shaking
  • automation compatible

Liquid Handling

Tempest Liquid Handler (x2)

Formulatrix

  • 6 reagent capacity
  • 0.2 - 5.0 ul dispense range
  • automated plate stackers (25 plate capacity)
Mantis Liquid Handler with LC3

Formulatrix

  • 28 reagent capacity
  • 0.1 - 5.0 ul dispense range
Cobra Nano Dispenser

Hudson Robotics

  • 0.2-100 uL aspiration
  • non-contact dispensing
  • 2 plate positions
  • on-deck tip cleaning
Nimbus Liquid Handler

Hamilton

  • 8 deck positions
  • 4 head spanning pipettor
  • plate gripper
MultiFlo FX Multimode Washer/Dispenser

BioTek

  • 2 peristaltic pumps
  • 2 syringe pumps
  • Random Access Dispense (RAD) module
  • Automated Media Exchange (AMX) module
  • Wash module for magnetic bead purification
OT-2 Liquid Handler (x2)

OpenTrons

  • programmable aspiration and dispensing
  • single and 8-channel pipette heads
Platemaster 96-Channel Pipette (x3)

Gilson

  • 2-220 uL capacity
  • 96 or 384 well plate formats
  • 4 deck positions
